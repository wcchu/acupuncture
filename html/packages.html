Web Design Packages

Standard Package - Starting at €500

The standard package is great for businesses and entrepreneuers who need a professional informational website to display their services and give customers a way to contact them online.

This package includes:

  • a professional template design
  • up to 5 web pages
  • one contact form
  • a newsletter sign-up box (if desired)

Standard Plus Package - Starting at €1000

The standard plus package is great for businesses who need a professional informational website that also gives the ability to display images of their products or services in a photo gallery.

This package includes:

  • all services in the standard package
  • up to 10 web pages
  • a photo gallery with unlimited categories and photos
  • an embedded food menu in graphic/pdf format

Platinum Package - Starting at €1500

The platinum package is great for businesses who need a professional website to sell their products or services.

This package includes:

  • all services in the standard plus package
  • unlimited number of web pages
  • a shopping cart with unlimited number of categories and products*, checkout process with payment, coupons and discount codes available

*up to 300 products will be uploaded at no additional charge.
